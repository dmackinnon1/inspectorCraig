The Case Files of Inspector Craig

Raymond Smullyan's book of puzzles "What is the Name of this Book?" introduced the logical detective Inspector Leslie Craig of Scotland Yard. On this page, Inspector Craig has provided some additional case files "for the benifit of those interested in the application of logic to the solution of crimes."

In each case history, the suspects can be deduced to be guilty or innocent, or it may happen that the evidence is not clear and their guilt or innocence remains uncertain.
