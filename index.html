The Case Files of Inspector Craig

Raymond Smullyan's book of logic puzzles "What is the Name of this Book?" introduced the detective Inspector Leslie Craig of Scotland Yard. On this page, Inspector Craig has provided some additional casefiles "for the benifit of those interested in the application of logic to the solution of crimes."

In each case history, the suspects can be deduced to be guilty or innocent, or it may happen that the evidence is not clear either way, and their guilt or innocense is uncertain.
